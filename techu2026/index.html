Why cloud

Three reasons IT is moving printing off the server rack.

01

Business agility

Scale up and down without procurement cycles. Faster time-to-market, less reliance on IT for day-to-day change.

02

IT efficiency

Ease of administration. Simple deployment and scaling. Connect from anywhere, by default secure.

03

Economy of scale

Low upfront investment. Pay as you go. OPEX instead of CAPEX, and no five-year refresh cycle.
